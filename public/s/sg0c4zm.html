Lincoln Barber Trades Haircuts for Diaper Donations to Help Struggling Families

Barber Sam Dov is swapping haircuts for diaper donations through June, aiming to collect $10,000 worth to distribute to struggling Lincoln families through the nonprofit Lincoln Littles.

Wetland Plant Once Endangered Recovers So Well It's Removed from Threatened Species List

The northeastern bulrush has recovered from just 13 populations in 1991 to 148 populations across eight states, leading the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service to remove it from the endangered species list.

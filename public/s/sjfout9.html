Red-Headed Woodpeckers and Rare Wildflowers Return to Ontario's Restored Prairies

After two decades of restoration, the Nature Conservancy of Canada has brought Red-headed Woodpeckers and rare wildflowers back to Ontario's Rice Lake Plains, rebuilding a vital tallgrass prairie ecosystem.

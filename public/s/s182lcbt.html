Vietnam's Secret Sanctuary Harbors 90% of World's Rarest Langurs

Vietnam's Ninh Binh mountains shelter more than 90 percent of the world's critically endangered Delacour's langurs, jet-black primates with distinctive white hip patches thriving through local conservation.

River Otters Are Swimming Back Into City Waterways Across the Chesapeake

River otters once endangered in the Chesapeake Bay region are swimming back into Washington D.C.'s Rock Creek Park, Baltimore's Inner Harbor, and Norfolk's Elizabeth River.

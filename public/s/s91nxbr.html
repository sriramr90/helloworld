Mi'kmaq Nation Reclaims Nearly 3,400 Acres of Traditional Lands

The Mi'kmaq Nation reclaimed Mesgilg Maqamigew, a nearly 3,400-acre parcel near Bridgewater that will more than double their territory and restore hunting and gathering grounds for future generations.

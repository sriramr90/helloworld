Mangrove Forests Worldwide Shift From Decline to Recovery After 40 Years

After losing 2,900 square kilometers over 30 years, the world's mangrove forests shifted to net gain around 2010, with closed-canopy mangroves growing nearly 20% healthier between the 1980s and 2023.

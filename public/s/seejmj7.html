Against the Odds: Kordofan Giraffes Make Stunning Comeback in Congo

Garamba National Park's Kordofan giraffes surged from 22 in 2012 to 105 today, with zero poaching since 2017—a stunning conservation triumph for Africa's most endangered subspecies.
